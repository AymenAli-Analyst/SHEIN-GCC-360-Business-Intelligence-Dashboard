# 🚀 SHEIN GCC Business Performance & Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

This Power BI project provides a comprehensive analysis of SHEIN's commercial performance, supply chain operations, and operational risk management across GCC markets between 2020 and 2025.

The dashboard was designed to help business stakeholders monitor KPIs, identify operational bottlenecks, evaluate market performance, and support strategic decision-making through interactive visual analytics.

# 🔍 Key Insights

### Revenue Performance

* Total Revenue exceeded 27 Billion.
* Saudi Arabia generated the highest revenue contribution.
* Revenue growth showed significant fluctuations between years.

### Supply Chain Performance

* Logistics expenses exceeded 1.19 Billion.
* Shipping volume increased significantly in recent years.
* Inventory accumulation was identified across multiple product categories.

### Operational Risk

* 84.49% of inventory was classified as healthy stock.
* Damaged inventory accounted for 7.18%.
* Expired inventory represented 2.99%.
* Several markets performed below revenue targets.

---

# ⚠️ Business Challenges

### Revenue Volatility

Revenue growth experienced multiple fluctuations which may affect long-term profitability.

### Market Concentration Risk

The business relies heavily on the Saudi Arabian market for revenue generation.

### Inventory Management Issues

High inventory levels increase carrying costs and risk of obsolescence.

### Operational Losses

Damaged and expired inventory contributes to unnecessary financial losses.

### Rising Logistics Costs

Transportation, insurance, and operational expenses continue to impact profitability.

---

# 💡 Recommendations

* Improve demand forecasting accuracy.
* Reduce damaged inventory below 3%.
* Reduce expired inventory below 1%.
* Optimize logistics and insurance expenses.
* Expand market penetration outside Saudi Arabia.
* Improve inventory turnover and replenishment strategies.
* Strengthen customer retention programs.
